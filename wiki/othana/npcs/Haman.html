Haman

One of the program leads of /othana/lore/project-legacy. It and Abimelech worked together on Smyrna for some time before having a violent disagreement, leading to Abimelech disabling Haman.

Now a "Reaver", Haman no longer uses an organic body.

Appearance: a bundle of rainbow light housed within a whirling sphere of hundreds of metal bands that can snap apart and reform, giving it arbitrarily many metal tendrils.
