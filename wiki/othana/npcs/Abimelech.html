Abimelech

(aka Verner Von Bluecher)

A lich like creature that lives in modern day. The BBEG(ish).

One of the program leads of Project Legacy. He and Haman worked together on /othana/locations/Smyrna/ for some time before having a violent disagreement, leading to Abimelech disabling Haman.
