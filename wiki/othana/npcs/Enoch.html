Enoch

(aka Alonzo The Merchant)

Pioneered reaver technology and consequently lead the charge on the technology to transfer mind into machine. In his final experiment, he transferred his own consciousness into a body that was supposed to be perfect. Access to extradimensional portals and other stranger powers. But the experiment didn't go as planned and parts of his body and mind were lost forever.
