A Promising New Result - Dragonborn Origin

outline

  • It had been 20 years since Pergumum was destroyed by the beam of radiant light (the sky was still scorched where the beam had pierced it)
  • Small merchant ship approaches the island with crew of 5 or 6, one of which (<digger>) is a new member that the captain is uncertain about
  • They’re here to collect the glass and other exotic materials that exist on the island. At this point it’s mostly picked clean, but <captain> still comes back when there’s no better other options
  • While collecting materials, the new member points out the gate and admits that she’s a digger with a license that could get them inside (this is why she came along in the first place)
  • Times have been rough so <captain> reluctantly agrees to explore it (against the law, but who’s to know)
  • They’re inside for hardly any time at all before they’re captured by Dragonborn (nobody has ever seen such creatures before)
  • They have some interaction/conversation during which <digger> plants the seeds of the idea of leaving this ruin (their whole world up till this point) for the larger world outside
  • The scurrying sound approaches which sends the Dragonborn running
  • The crew escapes their bonds using magic and decides to run as well
  • Being unfamiliar with this ruin, they end up in a chamber deeper in where they find a horrifying scene of a captured dragon
  • A conversation later with random bits of lore, they decide to free the dragon who makes its escape
  • ???? (is <digger> a Genasi? Uses their core refractor to escape?)
  • Crew narrowly escapes catching a brief glimse of the scurrying hive that lives deeper in the ruin
  • Some weeks later, a Dragonborn show up at Ephesus announcing that Pergamum is peopled again and would like to trade
