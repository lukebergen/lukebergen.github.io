data clips

Throughout Othana, bits of scrap exist that people have yet to find a purpose for. As a result, they are often melted down for raw material or just passed back and forth as a form of generic currency. Having no real purpose beyond this, nobody ever suspected the stories these bits of scrap contained. These are some of those stories.

Hunger for the Sky (regarding the nighttime earthquakes on Smyrna)

DataClip#f8e19e6b-4746-4404-a017-4c420c04e049

The door of the tavern bursts open and a towering house of a man steps inside, beaming. "I have a daughter! I'm a papa! Next round's on me!" the man exclaims to the cheers of the rest of the patrons. The man saunters to the bar barely able to contain his joy. He drops 10 scrap on the bar and nods to the man already pouring the drinks. "Congratulations, Haran", the barkeep says. "To her health", he toasts, raising one of the glasses. The barkeep continues pouring drinks for the rest of his customers as he pockets the scrap. One of which, is DataClip#f8e19e6b-4746-4404-a017-4c420c04e049 This is part of the story that it contains.
