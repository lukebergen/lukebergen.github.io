Slinky

HP: 12 AC: 12 Speed: 15 ft STR: 10 (+0) Dex: 14 (+2) CON: 10 (+0) INT: 6 (-2) WIS: 10 (+0) CHA: 6 (-2)

Purpose: Ancient roomba. Reason why ruins are generally pretty clean. Will attack if non-local in vacinity though.

Appearance: 4 foot long 8 inch radius slinky/spring looking thing. Looks like a cross between a sentient slinky and a snake.

Behavior: Simple creature that simply attacks the nearest local non-local If no non-locals are near by, it crawls around randomly

Actions:

  • Bash
    • Creature coils itself up before bursting out with a slamming attack on a creature within 5 feet
    • 1 d6 damage
  • Bite/Latch
    • 1 d4 damage
    • If successful, it latches onto the target and will move with the target wherever it goes
  • Restrain (while latched)
    • Basically just a grapple attempt
    • On success, the opponent is grappled and will take 1 d4 damage per round till it breaks free
