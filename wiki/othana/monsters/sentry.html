Sentry

HP: 50 AC: 14 Speed: 25 ft (5 feet if defense mode) STR: 14 (+2) Dex: 12 (+1) CON: 10 (+0) INT: 10 (+0) WIS: 10 (+0) CHA: 6 (-2)

Appearance: Bipedal robot with pincer like forearms Reference <file:../pics/sentry.jpg>

Behavior: Basically a more intense version of a Grunt but never sleeps and has different attack patterns

Actions:

  • Swipe
    • It slashes out with its pincer arms
    • 1 d8 damage
  • Sliding stab
    • Its arm starts spinning like a drill and it takes at least 10 feet of movement before stabbing out at its target
    • 2 d6 damage
  • Psychic blast
    • It rubs its two pincers together like a violinist but with a sharp cutting motion
    • Creatures within 80 foot radius must make DC 15 wisdom saving throw
      • on fail: take 2 d10 psychic damage and incur disadvantage on their next attack roll
      • on success: half damage and no disadvantage
  • Defense mode
    • bonus action
    • It opens its claws up and begins spinning them in a helicopter like way
    • Speed reduced to half
    • Imposes disadvantage on ranged attacks
    • While in this mode its only attack is a ramming attack where it basically just tries to smack you with hits helicoptering blades
      • rams forward (only 5 feet of movement), it does 4d4 slashing damage and knocks creature prone
  • Offense mode (bonus action)
    • Reverts out of defense mode
