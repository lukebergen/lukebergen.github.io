Baby Kraken

file:./kraken.jpg

HP: 200 (+ 10 tentacles with 25 hp each) AC: 16 (head) / 12 (tentacles) Speed: 20 ft, swim 40 ft (Tentacles are all -1 of head) STR: 18 (+4) DEX: 11 (+0) CON: 16 (+3) INT: 14 (+2) WIS: 12 (+1) CHA: 10 (+0)

Doing damage to a tentacle does damage to both the tentacle AND the base creature

While head is below sea, tentacle slaps have disadvantage but can try to break boat While head is above sea, tentacle slaps have no disadvantage but cannot try to break boat

If creature loses half its HP or 3 tentacles (whichever comes first) it disengages its tentacles and swims away

Actions (tentacles):

  • Slap:
    • Tentacle slaps a creature within 10 feet of its current location
    • 1 d6
  • Grapple:
    • Tentacle attempts to grapple a player
  • Fling (while grappling an enemy)
    • 1 d6 blugeoning and creature knocked prone

Actions (head):

  • Bite (only while head is surfaced)
    • Any creature within 15 feet of the head
    • Makes a lunging attack. 1d8
  • Crush (only while head is below water)
    • 1 d6 per tentacle on boat
    • uses action for all tentacles
  • Extend/Retract
    • Creature extends a tentacle out onto the boat and/or retracts a tentacle from the boat into the sea

Bonus actions:

  • Surface:
    • Head surfaces removing disadvantage to Slap but exposing creature to direct attack
    • Cannot attack boat while surfaced
  • Submerge:
    • Head goes back below sea
