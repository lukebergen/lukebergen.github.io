Boar

HP: 20 AC: 11 Speed: 40 ft STR: 13 (+1) DEX: 11 (+0) CON: 12 (+1) INT: 2 (-4) WIS: 9 (-1) CHA: 5 (-3)

Traits: Charge: If the boar moves at least 20 ft straight toward a target and then hits it with a tusk Attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 3 (1d6) slashing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 11 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

Relentless: If the boar takes 7 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 Hit Points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead. Recharges after a Short or Long Rest

Actions:

  • Tusk
    • Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) slashing damage.
