Eyes & Brute

Eyes HP: 10 AC: 10 Speed: 0 ft STR: 0 DEX: 0 CON: 0 INT: 0 WIS: 0 CHA: 0

Brute HP: 60 AC: 16 Speed: 20 ft STR: 14 (+2) DEX: 10 CON: 12 (+2) INT: 0 WIS: 0 CHA: 0

Behavior The Eyes and Brute work together in tandem and are always found as a set.

The Eyes take no actions and have no speed. Their only purpose is to keep track of the location of an enemy.

The Brute spends its turn mindlessly running into the nearest enemy. If its paired Eyes are destroyed or incapacitated it ceases all movement and action.

Action: On its turn, if it's paired eyes are aware of an enemy present, it will use its speed to run into the nearest such opponent.

Player makes a dex save. On success, the player is allowed to move to the left or right 5 feet and Brute misses. Otherwise...

  • 1d6 blugeoning damage
  • player is knocked back 5 feet
  • player makes a dex save (DC 12) on fail, is knocked prone
