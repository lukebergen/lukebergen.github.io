Sorter

HP: 4 AC: 10 Speed: 25 STR: 08 (-1) DEX: 12 (+1) CON: 08 (-1) INT: 06 (-2) WIS: 06 (-2) CHA: 06 (-2)

Purpose: Sift, identify, catalog, and distribute raw materials. Non-combative

Appearance: A dozen or so strips of metal forming a shaved cone kind of shape with a cubic diamond shaped "head". Where the cone ends a new upside down cone reaches down to the ground. It "stands" balanced on this bottom point. Like a d10 where one side was elongated with diamond perched atop it and where the strips meet at the middle instead of alternating triangles

Behavior: If any inorganic material comes within 60 feet it will scoot over, examine it, and if the material is of the type that this sorter is responsible for, it takes the material to the appropriate dispersement chamber, logs the material, and then deposits it.
