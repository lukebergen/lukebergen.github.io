Grunt

HP: 30 AC: 12 Speed: 25 ft STR: 10 (+0) Dex: 14 (+2) CON: 10 (+0) INT: 6 (-2) WIS: 10 (+0) CHA: 6 (-2)

Purpose: General purpose bot. They can be programmed to do a variety of tasks.

Appearance: A metalic egg with 4 medium cylinder multi-use mag-appendages and a head-like appendage

Default Dungeon Behavior: Simple sentry type creature. If nobody is around it will either patrol it's assigned territory or enter a low-power-use mode where it only listens/watches for movement before waking fully up to assess. If an "intruder" is detected it will attack on sight. Has basic target prioritization abilities

Actions:

  • Sliding bash
    • It makes at least 10 feet of movement before bashing into its target
    • 1 d6 damage
  • Tornado attack
    • It begins spinning rapidly in place and makes an attack against a creature within 5 feet
    • Reduces speed to 5 feet until it (as an action) chooses to stop spinning
    • 1 d8 damage
