  • BUSINESS
    • E: entrence from minor2 gate. Hallways south and east are heat warped
    • Assembly
      • burnt and melty. there are a couple warped /othana/monsters/grunts here
      • less hp, put up a fight but are clearly in a bad way. make it fight but should feel kind of sad
    • T: secret treasure room. Has a nice meaty refractor or component.
    • Construction
      • burnt and melty but less so than Assembly. 4 large melted blobs of assorted metal (arcana 14 check: factory-like assembling arms). a d6 of /othana/monsters/slinkys here
    • G: glass walls containing neat rows or various bits of metal and refractors shifting
    • Burdening
      • (N) 4 rows of metal block that looks similar to the walls but with additional small holes and refractor shaped recesses
        • investigation 8 check finds a small refractor
      • (D) an opening in the floor that goes down a flight of stairs leading to
        • a welded shut door (impassible) (self healing?)
    • Store room: 1 chest in middle of room, has some refractors. investigation check on shelving and such
  • RESIDENCE
    • ooms are 40x40 feet
    • Spaces between rooms are 40 feet
    • When moving between rooms a 1 on a d12 party encounters a slinky

    • F: burnt residence
      • same as a regular residence except have to break the door to get in
    • Park
      • massive building that once contained an underground park
      • ceiling looks different (faked sunlight)
      • in the center is a chest guarded by some /othana/monsters/sentrys and /othana/monsters/grunts
        • in the chest is an uncharged key
    • ZZZZZ: wall becomes grating through which players can see sewers beneath the city
