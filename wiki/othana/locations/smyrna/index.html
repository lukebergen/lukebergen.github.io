Smyrna

A simple farming island. Has two minor gates and a c-gate

  • history

City

Walled city with 4 quadrant districts It's square shaped but rotated 45 degrees

  • North quadrant is the civil district (mayors office, sheriffs office, etc...)
  • East quadrant is for housing livestock
  • South quadrant is the market district with shops, inn, healer, etc...
  • West quadrant is residential

Market District

  • Inn (run by /othana/npcs/Tamar)
    • Post for non-locals usually makes its way here for dispersal
  • General store (and mech) (run by /othana/npcs/Abigail and /othana/npcs/Eli)
  • Healer (run by /othana/npcs/Cyrus)

Government District

  • Mayors office
  • Sheriff's office
  • Post office
  • A nice park

Residental District

Assortment of houses. A couple small playgrounds here and there

Farmers District

Padocks, barns, etc...

Ruins

There are 3 ruins here, two minors and a c-level. They all work together toward general upkeep and are trying a mechanical approach to /othana/lore/project legacy. minor1 is generally the sorting and low level production. minor2 is the sciency bit for coming up with the test subjects and residency. c-gate is testing, QA, and final assessment by the chief architect.
