Genasi

  • The result of the Sardis cults' child sacrifice ritual
    • Twice a year, the Sardisians send a small child into their B-gate ruins
    • 50% of the time they're never seen again
    • 50% of the time they come back with fragmented memories as Genasi
  • They live to be about 200 (longer than canonical)
  • There is an expectation that they will spend some part of their lives wandering the world but that they will return
  • Almost always an air or water Genasi. Rarely a fire. Once an earth
