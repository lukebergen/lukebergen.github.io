Tritons

  • Children's creation myth
    • The Tritons were once mindless creatures wandering the sea
    • One day the land dwellers became a thing and god-of-sea summoned the Tritons to the region to protect the land dwellers
    • God-of-sea gave them intelligence, strength, etc... to help them protect the weak land dwellers
    • This is why the Tritons have a general sense of superiority/haughtiness
    • As a result, the Tritons see it as their duty to patrol the sea watching for landers in distress
      • ship wrecks, hostile sea creatures, etc...
    • Tritons have a racial trait where when in close proximity to ancient technology, they feel unease
      • The more powerful the tech/close to it they come, the greater the unease
      • Similar to a spider sense but more unpleasant and fear-oriented
        • For those Tritons who mingle with landers, this creates a dissonance
        • On the one hand, they (landers) are weak and in need of protection. But on the other hand they also blithely wander into and even use ancient tech
