Rooms

Also, scratch for some reason is in its own file... I forget how I was organizing this.

Rooms are generic areas that adventurers might potentially run into when exploring a gate.

Some rooms (many of them actuall) are unique to a particular gate

Processing Room

The room is quite large. Around the edges of the room thin metal rods jut out of the walls at irregular intervals

In the center of the room 6 rows of 8 foot cubes sit atop long tables. One of these rows has an extra cube. It is a mimic.

Residential District

Unique to Smyrna

This is a "super room" in that it contains many things including elements from other types of rooms

An enormous room in the shape of a cube. Unlike nearly all other rooms, this one is teaming with life. An artificial sun sits in the sky just below the highest point of the dome. This sun is (largely) powered by a rapidly spinning crystal on the ground at the center of the room. If destroyed (magical force field and a ton of HP and healing make this a very difficult task) the sun dims to become more moon-like in its light output.

Things that can be found in this room:

  • Residential buildings (flavor to taste) that act like "Residence" rooms

Residence

These are rooms that were used as residences by the ancients. They may contain nothing, lore-related things, treasures, or enemy encounters.
