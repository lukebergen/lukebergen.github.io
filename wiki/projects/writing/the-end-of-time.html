The End of Time

It’s dark. Not dark like the night. There are no stars or moon to light the streets. More like the kind of dark that an eclipse brings. It feels like it should be brighter, but isn’t. A pale, gray, monochrome.

Two figures walk through the street talking.

“Say Achy, wanna go down to the pier and watch the water?” “I suppose. Let’s have a bite first though.”
