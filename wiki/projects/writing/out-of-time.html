Out of Time

Beings out of time watch the earth timeline from 1900 onward. Time travel doesn't exist for us because constant loops have lead from bad/violent universes where time travel is discovered to more and more optimized universes where crime/violence are lesser and lesser. Eventually, the existence of time travel becomes a factor to be removed as part of the optimization toward the best timeline possible. Leaving us, at our universe. The End.

Apparently universes in which Hitlers and such exist are still better in comparison to whatever universes that have time travel look like?
