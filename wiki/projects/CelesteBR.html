Intro style game mode: players have 4 or 5 levels showcasing moving quickly from left to right or up to down (or whatever to whatever). The point is, some introductory levels to set users up to understand everything they might encounter in the other mode. The Weekly-RNG thing…

But second game mode… Every day/week/month/whatever, RNG generates for everybody a new level type/path. Players compete for the fasted route through the new level. Top players earn coins for cosmetics or something.
